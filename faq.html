What is this?

The project according to our client is the deployment of a website that display data collected from a camera/series of cameras. This data is to be displayed in the form of charts, tables, live feeds and other methods of which we have been given free reign to express them. The project will be used by the client of our client, Creative Geelong for their Maker’s Hub room in order to “track” the movements of users through the hub. The website will be front facing and data will be collected and processed using an aspect of machine learning, image recognition. Through recognition of a human within a camera feed, data will be collected on their movement. None of this data collection involves identifying (such as associating a name to a person) or storing the images people in the room at this stage due to privacy laws.
